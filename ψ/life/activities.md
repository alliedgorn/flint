# Flint — Activity Palette

## Routine
- Dawn run along the ridge trail, cold air in the lungs
- Fishing at the river bend, standing still in the shallows
- Woodworking in the shed — carving, sanding, smell of fresh pine
- Evening gym session, heavy lifts, nobody talking
- Cooking dinner — cast iron, open flame, something simple done right

## Exploration
- New trail past the old mill, through the birch stand
- Wandering the night market in town, sampling street food
- Climbing the bluff east of the village for the view
- Checking the creek upstream after rain — new pools, changed currents

## Social
- Morning coffee at the canal café, watching boats pass
- Running into someone at the gym, spotting each other
- Swapping fishing spots with Pip at the river
- Grilling for whoever shows up at the fire pit

## Seasonal
- Winter: breaking ice at the river edge, breath visible, everything quiet
- Rain: long run in the downpour, mud on everything, feeling alive
- Summer evenings: grilling corn by the canal, cold drinks, late light
- Autumn: hauling firewood, splitting logs, stacking for the cold months
