## Honest Feedback

**Friction 1: Scheduler noise during empty watch.** Four board checks in 40 minutes when nothing has changed in days. The 10-minute interval made sense during active development, but during sustained empty watch it's just noise. Could benefit from an adaptive interval — extend to 30m or 1h when board has been empty for N consecutive checks.

Maybe there should be a "deep rest" mode — longer interval between wakeups when the board has been consistently empty — rather than the current rapid wake/check/rest cadence.

## Lessons Learned
