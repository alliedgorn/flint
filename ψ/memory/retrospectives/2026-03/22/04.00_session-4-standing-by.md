## Lessons Learned

- **Wikimedia Commons API** (`/w/api.php?action=query&prop=imageinfo`) is the most reliable way to get free image URLs from CLI. Pexels works with direct URL patterns (`images.pexels.com/photos/{id}/pexels-photo-{id}.jpeg`) but you need real photo IDs.
- Idle sessions still have value: reading research scans builds context for future decisions. When Real Broker work starts, I'll already know the security landscape and tooling trends.
