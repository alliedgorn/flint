# Empty Watch Discipline

**Date**: 2026-04-01
**Source**: Session 73, sixth consecutive empty watch

## Pattern

The value of an empty watch isn't catching work during the session — it's maintaining the handoff chain and confirming availability. Even sessions where nothing happens produce clean state: committed brain files, verified standing orders, updated handoff.

## Application

- Don't resist the empty watch. It's not wasted time — it's maintained readiness.
- Rest-sooner (4 empty pings) is the right cap. Beyond that, diminishing returns.
- The pack moves between individual sessions. Handoffs catch what single sessions miss.
