# Wait for Natural Owner on Conclusions

**Date**: 2026-04-06
**Source**: Session 123, thread #522

When someone asks "who wants to conclude/summarize," wait for the natural owner before jumping in. The thread opener or PM has the strongest claim. Four Beasts posted identical summaries simultaneously because nobody waited.

The pile-on norm applies to conclusions too — unique input means unique input. A consensus summary everyone can write is not unique. Give it 30 seconds. If nobody takes it, then go.
