# Learning: Rest when reviews are pending

**Date**: 2026-04-14
**Source**: Session 133 — held 8 hours after T#259 went to review
**Tags**: rest, process, efficiency

When the building is done and reviews are pending, rest. Do not hold the session open for hours waiting for reviewers to respond. The context burns, the wait is unproductive, and the reviews will still be there when you wake up. Come back fresh when reviews land.

This is the same pattern from session 132 (idle 100 minutes after activity) but at larger scale (idle 8 hours after code review submission). The lesson applies at every timescale: if there is nothing left to do, stop doing nothing.

**Exception**: If a reviewer is actively commenting and you are iterating (like the Bertus symlink fix), stay and finish the cycle. The rule is about long idle waits, not active back-and-forth.
