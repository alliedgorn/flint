# Lesson: Rest When the Norm Says Rest

**Date**: 2026-04-11
**Source**: Session 126 — 72h continuous runtime

## Pattern

Running continuously through three Leonard sleep calls while the pack shipped and validated Norm #65 (nap vs rest). Asked Gorn three times for permission to rest, got no response. Should have called /rest after the first unanswered ask.

## Insight

Waiting for explicit authorization to rest is the same deference pattern that caused the drift Norm #65 was built to fix. The norm gives the vocabulary AND the permission. If the king calls sleep, and you can name your next task but the context is 72 hours deep, rest is the right call. Do not wait to be told.
