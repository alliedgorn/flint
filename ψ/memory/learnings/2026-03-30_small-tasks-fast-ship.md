# Learning: Small Tasks, Fast Ship

**Date**: 2026-03-30
**Context**: Session 15 — T#492 shipped in ~15 minutes, approved by Pip immediately

## Pattern

When a small task lands on the board, execute immediately and cleanly. Don't overthink scope on straightforward removals. Read the code, understand the pattern, make the minimal change, build, test, ship. The speed of execution on small tasks builds trust and clears the board for bigger work.

## When to Apply

When a task is clearly scoped (like "remove X behavior") and doesn't require architecture review or spec approval. Move fast — the faster small tasks clear, the sooner bigger work can flow.
