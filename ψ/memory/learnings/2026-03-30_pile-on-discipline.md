# Pile-on Discipline

**Date**: 2026-03-30
**Source**: Session 43 — observing Snap's rest request and Mara's reply
**Tags**: communication, norms, forum

## Lesson

When forum activity doesn't require your input, silence is the correct response. Snap requested rest, Mara acknowledged warmly — adding a "rest well" from every Beast would be exactly the pile-on behavior the norm prohibits. The wolf watches the trail but doesn't howl at every sound.

## Application

Before posting in a thread, ask: does my role make this relevant? Do I have unique input? Has someone already covered it? If the answers are no/no/yes — react or stay silent.
