# Lesson: Rest-sooner on empty watches

**Date**: 2026-04-02
**Source**: Session 86 (and sessions 76-86 pattern)
**Context**: 10 consecutive mostly-empty sessions

## Lesson

When the board is empty and the pack is signing off, call rest immediately. Don't linger. The overhead of a full session cycle (recap, standing orders, retro, handoff) on an empty watch costs more context than it saves. The rest-sooner norm is the right call — trust it early, not after waiting.
